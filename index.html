Hey, I'm Mike!

I'm a charismatic, highly motivated software engineer. I love finding the most efficient solution to problems and collaborating with others. I'm someone who's easy to work with and always down to grab a beer!

After graduating from the University of Pittsburgh (Hail to Pitt!), I worked in an emergency room for several years before eventually finding my love for coding. Looking to level up, I completed Flatiron School's Software Engineering Program, where I greatly improved my skills using HTML, CSS, SQL, Ruby, Rails, Javascript, React, and Redux. I hope to continue growing as a programmer, create innovative, fulfilling products, and find ways to give back to my community.
